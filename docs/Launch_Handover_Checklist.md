# Launch and Handover Checklist

## Launch Readiness
- Final release candidate tagged.
- Staging smoke checks passed.
- Route regression tests passed.
- Deployment rollback plan reviewed.

## Operational Runbooks Published
- Deployment runbook
- Incident response playbook
- Secret management baseline
- Moderation and support procedures

## Ownership Handover
- Confirm owners for:
  - deployment operations
  - moderation queue
  - billing incidents
  - customer support escalations
- Confirm on-call rota and escalation path.

## 30-Day KPI Baseline
- Define target metrics:
  - active projects
  - provider response speed
  - conversion to contacted/quoted/won lead stages
  - moderation backlog age
- Capture day-0 baseline values.

## Go/No-Go Decision
- Final stakeholder signoff collected.
- Known risks and mitigations acknowledged.
- Launch window approved.
