Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

AskMarley ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services (the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Registration: Name, email address, phone number, UK postcode, and account password
  • Service Requests: Details of services you're looking for, location information, and chat messages
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely via Stripe)
  • Feedback: Communications, surveys, and feedback you submit to us

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (device type, operating system, browser type)
  • Postcode location data (for matching providers)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, interactions)
  • IP address and cookie information

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Provider verification records (for provider quality assurance)
  • Postcode directory data (for geographic matching)
  • Analytics from third-party services

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving the Service
  • Matching you with appropriate service providers based on your needs and location
  • Processing transactions and sending related information
  • Communicating with you about service updates and changes
  • Conducting analytics to understand usage patterns
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For UK/EU residents: We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to provide our services
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving our Service, fraud prevention, and analytics
  • Consent: Marketing communications (with your explicit consent)
  • Legal Obligation: Complying with UK and EU law

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With verified professionals you've requested to contact
  • Payment Processors: Stripe (for secure payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or court order
  • Business Transfers: In the event of merger, acquisition, or business sale

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as:

  • You maintain an active account with us
  • Necessary to provide our services
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

7. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to exceptions)
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@askmarley.co.uk

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Encryption of personal data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data

No method of transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand usage patterns
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

10. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@askmarley.co.uk
  • Address: AskMarley Limited, UK
  • Data Protection Officer: Available upon request

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version. We will notify you of material changes via email or prominent notice on the Service.
